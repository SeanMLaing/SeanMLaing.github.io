My Rohit Experience

My Rohit Experience was a prototype page I was tasked with creating in the time that I worked at Rohit Group of Companies. The site would serve as a tracker for new home purchases and would be used by both sales people and the new home owners to easily and visually step through the process of handing over the keys to their new home. The base purpose of the site was to track how happy customers were with the purchase process and ensure the sales person had taken them through each step. Along the way of checking off each step in the process the cusomter would fill out a quick survey powered by SurveyMonkey and using the SurveyMonkey API the score would be passed back to the sales manager. The site was overseen by the marketing department and every week or so they would look over the prototype and see about adding or removing features.

The site is writen in PHP and uses an internal API to populate the units and unit info. The surveys are done through SurveyMonkey although since the developer key has expired it will not update the step as complete after doing the survey.
